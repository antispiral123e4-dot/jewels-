FAQ / Care guide

Keep the shine happy

How do I size my bracelet?

Measure your wrist, add a half inch for comfort, and message us if you want a custom fit.

Can the beads get wet?

A splash is fine. For longer shine, take them off before swimming, lotions, or heavy perfume.

What are shipping and returns like?

Orders ship in 2-4 business days. Unworn ready-made pieces can be returned within 21 days.
